Trash Bot

Robotic waste collector with smart detection, sorting, and dual operation modes

Project by Ayush Chakraboty
Overview

Trash Bot is an autonomous and remotely controlled robot designed to detect, collect, and sort rubbish into appropriate bins. It integrates sensors, a robotic arm, and classification logic to make waste management efficient and eco-friendly.

Key Features

  • Automatic detection of rubbish and navigation to the target.
  • Sensor-based alignment before pickup.
  • Robotic arm for lifting and placing trash.
  • Sorting mechanism for metallic and non-metallic waste.
  • Operates autonomously or via Bluetooth remote control.

How It Works

Using a combination of vision/sensors, Trash Bot identifies rubbish. Once detected, it approaches the trash, aligns itself, and deploys its robotic arm to collect it. The onboard sorter analyzes the material type and deposits it into the correct bin (metallic or non-metallic). Users can switch between automatic mode and Bluetooth-controlled mode for manual operation.

Sensing
